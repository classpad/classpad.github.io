Classpad resources

A compilation of classpad programs and functions used by me in Year 12!

Why am I revealing this content?
Using programs and eActivities can solve 'recurring' or 'expected' questions, which saves a large amount of time during a test that can be spent on harder/novel/unique questions.
Additionally, classpad programs can be an extension to your notes. The classpad has a capacity of 5.5 MB for eActivities alone - in practice, this translates to infinitely many pages of notes. Unlike the two pages of hardcopy notes you bring into a test or exam, the 'notes' of the classpad are interactive.
Personally, for me the classpad was a good way to put my programming skills to use.
